Marketing is all about attracting new users and giving user’s quality content that will want them to use the app on a regular basis. The brand will be made up of attributes, market positioning, targeted customers, iconology, a logo, tagline etc. Every customer will have their own unique needs. We provide a variety of options that include social media, co-marketing, SEO, pay for advertising, PR, shareability and so on.

Everything we do, we have the end user journey at the fore front of our minds. The user experience is our number one priority. Our aim is to make everything as simple as it can be, as intuitive as possible and this covers all aspects of an app from on-boarding to registration to general usage and navigation. First, we build our apps and websites in wireframes before handing over to the designers.

All websites and apps need to be dynamic, responsive and scalable. We examine all of the relevant growth aspects before we move into the development phase. A well designed architect plan is looking forward at growth challenges and examining how best to overcome potential user bottlenecks.

We develop websites and native apps that can be downloaded from both the Apple and Google App stores. Nothing is complete until we have thoroughly tested it! This includes internal alpha testing as well as user beta testing. When we’re happy with all of the results we can go into the deployment phase.

We submit the apps to both Google Play and the Apple App store. Once we get the all clear the app is available to be downloaded to a smartphone or tablet and we let loose on discoverability and marketing.

We believe in a balance between a good looking app and a simple to navigate user experience. Our in house design team turn the initial wireframes into mock ups with various different samples of what the app will look like when it is completed. At this stage we flesh out aspects such as colour schemes, logos, styles & fonts. The designs and user experience are tested by users prior to any development work commencing.
